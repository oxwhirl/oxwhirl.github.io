### WhiRL Reading Group

The WhiRL Reading Group is held on **Tuesdays at 15:00** every other week (mostly in person) and is open to everyone.  To add your email to the mailing list, please contact [Zilin Wang](mailto:zilin.wang@lmh.ox.ac.uk). Furthermore, if you interested in giving a talk, feel free to reach out to us at any time!
